How old are you?

Introducing another ta's- 'how many...?' pattern with -puna' 'be ... years old' to show learners how to ask and give ages. Reinforces the 'me' and 'you' endings, as well as all the number patterns, including the older, now idiomatic element metla's- for 'ten'. Images use photos of people matching the age said: baby-toddler-kid-older kid-young adult-adult-older adult
