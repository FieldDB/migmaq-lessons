Identifying grandparents

Introducing grandparents, reinforcing earlier-introduced patterns.

Taluisit gmijgamij?

What is your grandfather's name?

- Nmijgamij Piel teluisit.

My grandfather's name is Peter.

Taluisit gugmij?

What is your grandmother's name?

- Nugumij Nastasi teluisit.

My grandmother's name is Anastasia.
