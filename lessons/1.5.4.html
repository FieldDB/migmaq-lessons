When were you born?

Reviewing 'me' and 'you' past forms, through their natural conversational use around asking the month of someone's birth.

Ta'n ugs'tqamui's'p?

When were you born?

- Ugs'tqamuiap a...'Sqoljuigu's. Gat gi'l?

I was born in uh...May. And you?

Ni'n ugs'tqamuiap a... Nipnigu's.

I was born in uh...June.
