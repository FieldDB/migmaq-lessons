Speaking Native

Introducing 'speak ...' verb in the form of 'speak Native'. Pointing out that 'speak Native' is the more common/natural way for Mi'gmaq speaks to convey what would usually be said in English as 'speak Mi'gmaq'. Also briefly introducing nige' 'now' and getu'- 'want to, going to', and the -nej 'let's...' patterns.

Nnui'sin gi'l?

Do you speak Native [lit. 'speak Native', but used where English would say 'speak Mi'gmaw']

- E'e, 'nnui'si.

Yes, I speak Native.

Getu'-nnuisin nige'?

Do you want to speak Native now?

- E'e, 'nnui'sinej.

Yes, let's speak Native.
