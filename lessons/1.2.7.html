Identifying uncle, aunt, cousins

Three related kinship terms---uncle, aunt, and cousin---are introduced, plus a minimal introduction to 'many' and 'one'. Word order for informational importance/prominence is again demonstrated.

Taluisit 'gtlamugsis?

What is your uncle's name?

- Ntlamugsis Ta'pit teluisit.

My uncle's name is David.

Taluisit 'gsugwis?

What is your aunt's name?

- 'Nsugwis Agn's teluisit.

My aunt's name is Agnes.

Gatu go'gmaw?

And your cousin?

- Pugwelgig no'gmaq, gatu newte'jit teluisit Poligap.

I have lots of cousins [lit. 'many are my cousins'], but one is named Polycarpe.
