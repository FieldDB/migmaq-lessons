Work/study

Reviewing /tami (tett).../ 'where...' pattern, reviewing endings for 'me' and 'you', introducing 'work' and 'study/learn', and briefly, several other important patterns: mu ...-w 'I ... not ...', etl- 'be doing...'

Tami tett etllugwen?

Where do you work?

- Mu elugwew, etl-gina'masi.

I don't work, I'm a student [lit. 'I am studying/learning']

Goqwei etl-gina'masin?

What are you studying?

- 'Nnui'suti etl-gina'masi.

I am studying Mi'gmaq [lit. 'Native language'].
