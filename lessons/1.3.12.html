Who is that woman standing at the rock?

Ji'nm nemi'g, gaqamit awtigtug. Wen na?

I see a man, standing in the road. Who is that?

- So'se'p na. Negm na 'njignam.

That's Joseph. He's my younger brother.

Wen na ala e'pite's gaqamit gun'tewigtug?

Who is that young woman standing at the rock?

- Malia'n na. Negm na 'ngwe'ji'j.

That's Mary-Ann. She is my younger sister.

Wen na ula ji'nm napui'gnigtug?

Who is this man in the picture?

- Ula na 'nsis. Mise'l teluisit.

This is my older brother. His name is Michael.
