Who is that?

Wen na ala ji'nm gaqamit awtigtug?

Who is that man standing in the road?

- Ala ji'nm a? Mu nenuaq.

That man? I don't know him.

Wen na ala e'pit pemgopit patautigtig?

Who is that woman sitting at the table?

- Negm elg mu nenuaq. Amgwesewei tett pegisin, me' mu wen nenuaq.

Her too I don't know. It's the first time I've come here; I still don't know anyone.

Wen na ula napui'gnigtug?

Who is this in the picture?

- Ni'n na.

It's me.
