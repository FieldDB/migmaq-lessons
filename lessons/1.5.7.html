What date is your birthday?

This is a simple review lesson, combining talking about birthdays with the naming of dates. In particular, it reworks the previous lesson's list-based material in the form of an actual simple conversation. It also brings to the front the use of gatu 'but, however; as for...' in its 'as for...' topic-shifting sense. Again use the exact calendar date pictures for each speaker.

Ta'sugunit gi'l aji-puna'n?

What date do you have your birthday on?

- Ni'n aji-puna'i tapuisgegugunij jel si'st. Gatu gi'l?

I have my birthday on the 23rd. And you?

Ni'n aji-puna'i metla'sugunij jel newt.

I have my birthday on the 11th.
