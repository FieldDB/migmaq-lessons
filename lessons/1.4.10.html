How many people? 1-12.

This lesson combines the patterns of the original minimal [1, 2, 3, many] people-counting system with the above higher number patterns to show learners how to count up to ninety-nine people.

Ta'sijig ji'nmug?

How many men are there?

- Newte'jit. Newte'jit ji'nm.

One. One man.
