Identifying things

This lesson introduces the fundamental 'what is this/that?' question so basic to learning a language THROUGH the language. It also reinforces the previously introduced /X na Y/ pattern as an effective equivalent to English 'X is Y, X = Y'. It requires pictures, better if video, to see the near (ula) and far (ala), and which people the speakers are looking at.

Goqwei na ula?

What is this?

- Ula na ga'qan.

This is a door.

Goqwei na ala?

What is that?

- Ala na tuo'p'ti.

This is a window.
