Afraid to speak Native

Building on the preceding lesson, but now to address the most common problem faced by learners: embarrassment or fear of being laughed at. Also introduces 'everybody' and some basic terms for feelings; these will all be taught again in more detail in later lessons.

Nnui'sin gi'l?

Do you speak Native?

- E'e, 'nnui'si.

Yes, I speak Native

Getu'-nnui'sin nige'?

Do you want to speak Native now?

- Jipatm nnui'sin, muta maligamiten.

I'm afraid to speak Native, because I will be laughed at.

'Ms't wen wijei telo'teget, amgwesewei.

Everyone feels that way, at first.
