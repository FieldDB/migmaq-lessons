How many people do you see?

This lesson combines the earlier nemi'g(ig)/nemitu(ann) pattern with the 'how many ...?' patterns just shown. As in earlier lessons, the main aim is helping learners see how they can now build more complex sentences. Image files again simply show these numbers: X number of countables together, either as a natural picture or a photoshopping of X number of identical images.

Ta'sijig nemi'jig ji'nmug?

How many men do you see?

- Nemi'g newte'jit ji'nm.

I see one man.

Ta'sijig nemi'jig ji'nmug?

How many men do you see?

- Nemi'gig tapusijig ji'nmug.

I see two men.

Ta'sijig nemi'jig ji'nmug?

How many men do you see?

- Nemi'gig ne'sisijig ji'nmug.

I see three men.

Ta'sijig nemi'jig ji'nmug?

How many men do you see?

- Nemi'gig pugwelgig ji'nmug.

I see many men.
