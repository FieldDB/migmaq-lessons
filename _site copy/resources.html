Twitter

Listuguj Mi'gmaq language program's Twitter account. Bridges technology and tradition to keep the language alive and well in communities and on the web.

Facebook

A Facebook group for Mi'gmaq learners and speakers in Listuguj. Members meet to practice conversational Mi'gmaq in Listuguj.
